About The Greenhouse

Connect with fellow gardeners and share fresh, local produce in our community marketplace.

What is The Greenhouse?

The Greenhouse is a platform for connecting with other gardeners and sharing your own fruits and vegetables with others! You can also get some fresh produce from other gardeners, or just connect with others who are interested in gardening IN REAL LIFE! Our mission is to help our community become more self-sufficient and connected with each other through the means of something simple: gardening!

How does The Greenhouse work?

On The Greenhouse, you can create an account and host a marketplace with others! Just click "Create Event" to host your own marketplace, or browse events and join them to get some fresh produce from other gardeners! You can either choose to buy or sell items, or both! When you join an event, you can let the host know the likelihood that you'll be attending, and what you'll be bringing if you choose to sell!

Who can use The Greenhouse?

The Greenhouse is a community for anyone who is interested in gardening, wants to get some fresh produce from other gardeners, or is just looking for a place to connect with others! It's completely free to join and use, and anyone can join!

How can I use The Greenhouse?

The first step is to create an account! Once you've created an account, you can start hosting events and browsing events to join! You can also view your account page to see your events and event attendance, and to update your profile information. Signing up is completely free, so there's no reason not to try it out!
